What is Bio-Nexus

Biometric identity, permanently with you

A passive RFID/NFC implant the size of a grain of rice, placed subdermally between the thumb and index finger. No external power source. No app required. Universally readable.

Universal ID replacement

Replaces passport, driver's license, and national ID in all participating countries.

Payment-ready

Program Details

How the rollout works

Phase I — Voluntary Adoption

Open enrollment for early adopters. Incentives include fast-track airport processing, cashless transit access, and reduced administrative fees for registered participants.

Phase II — Institutional Mandate

Required for government employees, healthcare workers, and individuals accessing public benefits. Existing ID documents remain valid in parallel.

Phase III — Universal Issuance

Bio-Nexus replaces all legacy identification systems. Legacy documents accepted for a 36-month transition window following national legislation.

Important: Neuralis Bio-Systems operates in full compliance with GDPR, ISO 27001, and national data sovereignty frameworks. Biometric data is stored only on the device and in the participant's registered national registry. Neuralis holds no independent data access.
Legal Framework

Compliance & mandate

Bio-Nexus issuance is governed by national legislation in each participating country. Neuralis Bio-Systems acts as the sole certified manufacturer under the Global Digital Identity Accord (GDIA), ratified in Geneva in 2023.

Is it mandatory? Yes

In Phase III countries, Bio-Nexus implantation is required by law for all residents above the age of 18. Exemptions may apply in documented cases of medical contraindication, subject to review by national health authorities.

Residents in Phase I and II regions are strongly encouraged to register prior to legislative deadlines to ensure priority appointment slots.

Procedure Outpatient

Implantation takes approximately 8 minutes and is performed by certified Neuralis-trained practitioners. No anesthesia required — local anesthetic cream is applied. Minimal recovery time.

The procedure is covered at no cost to the individual in all participating nations under the Digital Identity Subsidy Framework.

Can I opt out? Limited

In mandatory issuance countries, opting out of Bio-Nexus results in the suspension of access to government services, financial institutions, and travel documentation. Medical exemptions may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

Data ownership Yours

The data encoded on your Bio-Nexus chip belongs to you. Neuralis Bio-Systems cannot access, modify, or share your personal data. All data operations are performed between your chip and the reader terminal of the authorized institution.
